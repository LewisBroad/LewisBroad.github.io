My Projects

Project 1: Funky Ride

My first project was made in unity. It was a racing game. We were graded on the overall level design and not just the coding elements of the level. We were encouraged to use the asset store using assets that fit our overall theme. I created a race track in the country with a lake, forest and mountains. I enjoyed decorating the map with models such as the hot air balloons, fan stands, emergency vehicles and a penguin.

Project 2: Baldy vs Zombie2

I made a 2D zombie shooter game in which we were graded mainly on the coding. The game uses pooling for the spawning of enemies and bullets. The game is timed and ends on the characters health hitting 0 or the time. The aim is to eliminate as many zombies as possible in the given time. An issue I had with the game was that the projectiles fired from the player would collide with the player and so sometimes would seemingly not fire. To fix this, I had a separate entity for the gun that follows the player and fires from that.

Unreal Engine: Fight Lab

In my first project in unreal engine, me and some others in my class created a melee combat game. I was in charge of the code of the game, the levels and the gameplay. I created the AI which used the same animation and fighting mechanics as the player and had the instructions to walk towards the player and attack when close. The aim is to defeat all the enemies and that will allow the player to proceed through a portal.

Game Jam

During my studies, I participated in a game jam along with other students. The theme was "make me laugh". I, along with another student handled the coding in our game whereas another two created the sound and the sprites. My main part was the boss fight. The player will be locked into the boss fight where the boss will shoot normal bullets at the player. Upon hitting half health, the boss' speed will double and they will shoot homing missiles at the player.

Animation

In addition to developing games, I have also been learning animation. I had been tasked with making animations using IK constraints, parent and child linking, and character rigging. I learned to use Maya in this time to create animations but decided to create my animation in Unreal Engine 5 as I wanted to enhance my skills in this engine. I used face tracking and manually rigged the movement of the characters in my animation.
